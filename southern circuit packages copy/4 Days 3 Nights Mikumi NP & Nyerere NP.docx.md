  
**4 Days/3 Nights Mikumi National Park & Nyerere National Park**

**Day 1: Catch SGR Train, Depart to Mikumi National Park for A Game Drive**

* Catch an earliest SGR Train at 6:00 am, and depart to Town of Morogoro, arriving at 7:40 am.  
* **Departure to Mikumi:** On arrival, picked up by your driver-guide and depart to Mikumi National (approx. 2 hours’ drive).  
* **Game Drive:** Explore Mikumi National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Camp Bastian**   
* **Meal Plan:** lunch and dinner 

**Day 2: Depart to Nyerere National Park (Selous) and An Evening Boat Safari**

* **Departure to Nyerere National Park:** Depart to Nyerere National Park (approx. 4 \- 5 hours’ drive) arriving in the afternoon.  
* **Evening Boat Safari:** Explore the Rufiji River through a fascinating boat safari experience to encounter the aquatic animals, mostly crocodiles and hippos, while enjoying a rich number of bird-life found in the park.   
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Nyerere Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner


**Day 3: Full Day Game Drive at Nyerere National Park (Selous Game Reserve)**

* **Full Day Game Drive:**  Explore Nyerere National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Nyerere Tented Camp**   
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 4: An Early Morning Walking Safari & Departure to Dar es Salaam**

* **Morning Walking Safari:** Creep silently along paths swept by the feet of large animals to discover secret watering holes, watch as families of elephant wallow and play entirely oblivious to your presence.  
* **Departure to Dar es Salaam:** Depart back to Dar es Salaam (approx. 3 \- 4 hours’ drive) arriving in the early afternoon.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
