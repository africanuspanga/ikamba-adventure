**14 Days/13 Nights Kigoma, Gombe National Park, Katavi National Park, Lake Shore – Lake Tanganyika, Mbeya, Isimila Stone Age Site, Ruaha National Park & Town of Iringa**

**Day 1: Fly to Kigoma and Transfer to A Hotel**

* **Flight to Kigoma:** Transfer to the Airport to catch your flight at 14:30 hrs and fly to Town of Kigoma, arriving at 17:00 hrs. On your arrival at the airport, you’ll be picked up by your driver guide and transferred to a hotel.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Lake Tanganyika Hotel**  
* **Meal Plan:** lunch and dinner

**Day 2: Boat Transfer to Gombe National Park for Chimpanzee Trekking**

* **Car Transfer to Kibirizi Village:** Picked up by your driver guide and transfer to Kibirizi Village (approx. 30 – 40 minutes’ drive)  
* **Boat Transfer to Gombe National Park:** Then catch a private boat at 8:00 am and transfer to Gombe National Park (approx. 1 – 2 hours)  
* **Chimp Trekking at Gombe National Park:** Guided hike to experience Jane Goodall’s famous chimp communities alongside other primates like baboons and monkeys.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Kasekela Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 3: Full Day Chimp Trekking at Gombe National Park**

* **Full Day Chimp Trekking at Gombe:** Explore the Gombe National Park on-foot and experience its nature while trekking its chimps, birdlife, rainforests and swim on the world’s second deepest Lake Tanganyika.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Kasekela Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 4: Boat Transfer to Kigoma and Depart to Katavi National Park**

* **Boat Transfer to Kigoma:** Catch an earliest private boat at 8:00 am and depart to Kigoma Town (approx. 1 \-2 hours)  
* **Depart to Katavi National Park:** Depart to Katavi National Park while passing through various beautiful scenery, town and villages on the way (approx. 7 hours drive), arriving in the evening.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Katavi Wildlife Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 5: Full Day Game Drive at Katavi National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive at Katavi:** Explore the raw wilderness with vast herds of buffalo and elephants, massive hippo and crocodile congregations in drying riverbeds and diverse habitats.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Katavi Wildlife Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner


**Day 6: Full Day Game Drive at Katavi National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive at Katavi:** Enjoy another full day game drive through the enormous and rich wildlife of the Katavi National Park until and its stunning sunset.   
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Katavi Wildlife Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 7: Depart to Lake Shore and Relax at the Shores of Lake Tanganyika**

* **Depart to Lake Shore:** Depart to Lake Shore (approx. 2 – 3 hours’ drive). Check-in at the lodge on arrival while your hot lunch is served. Later relax and unwind at the beautiful beaches of the world’s second deepest lake \- Lake Tanganyika with a great view of its sunset in the evening.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Lake Shore Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 8: Activities, Relax and Unwind at Lake Tanganyika**

* **Activities and Unwind at the Beach:** At your own space and time, enjoy and experience various activities at Lake Tanganyika, including kayaking, diving, snorkeling, mountain biking, waterskiing and sunset cruise.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Lake Shore Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 9: Full Day Relaxation at the Shores of Lake Tanganyika**

* **Relax and Unwind at the Beach:** In this day, you can relax and unwind, swim at the lake, while enjoying some of the services offered by the lodge at your liking and your own space, such services include spa and massage, practicing yoga and meditation, visiting the Historical Old Kipili Church.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Lake Shore Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 10: Depart to Town of Mbeya and Transfer to A Hotel**

* **Depart to Town of Mbeya:** Depart to Town of Mbeya and explore various scenes of the beautiful villages and town, communities and cultures (approx. 7 – 8 hours drive), arriving in the evening.   
* **Accommodation:** Transfer to a hotel, and spend the overnight at **Desderia Hotel**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 11: Visit Isimila Stone Age Historical Site and Depart to Ruaha National Park**

* **Visit the Isimila Stone Age Site:** Depart to Isimila Stone Age Site (approx. 3 hours drive)   
* **Isimila Stone Age Site:** It’s one of Tanzania’s most fascinating archaeological treasures. Here, guided by an expert, explore ancient sandstone pillars and rock formations that date back hundreds of thousands of years, offering a rare glimpse into early human history.  
* **Departure to Ruaha National Park:** Later, depart to Ruaha National Park (approx. 2 \- 3 hours’ drive), arriving in the early evening.  
* **Accommodation**: Overnight at **Ruaha Hill Top Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 12: Full Day Game Drive at Ruaha National Park** 

* **Full Day Game Drive at Ruaha:** Explore Ruaha National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, herds of elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Ruaha Hill Top Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 13: Full Day Game Drive at Ruaha National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive at Ruaha:** You’ll experience and enjoy another full day game drive at the enormous Ruaha National Park early evening.   
* **Departure to Town of Iringa:** Later, depart to Town of Iringa (approx. 2 \- 3 hours’ drive)  
* **Accommodation**: Overnight at **Iringa Sunset Hotel**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 14: Depart to Morogoro, Catch SGR Train and Depart to Dar es Salaam**

* **Depart to Morogoro:** Depart to Town of Morogoro (approx. 5 – 6 hours’ drive)  
* **SGR Train Station and Depart to Dar es Salaam:** Upon arrival at Morogoro, catch your train at 16:25 hrs. and depart to Dar es Salaam, arriving at 18:10 hrs., and transferred to your hotel.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
