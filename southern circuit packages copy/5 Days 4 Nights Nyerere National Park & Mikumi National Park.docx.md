**5 Days/4 Nights Nyerere National Park & Mikumi National Park**

**Day 1: Depart to Nyerere National Park and An Evening Boat Safari**

* **Departure:** Depart from Dar es Salaam and drive to Nyerere National Park (approx. 3 \- 4 hours drive).   
* **Evening Boat Safari:** Explore the Rufiji River through a fascinating boat safari experience to encounter the aquatic animals, mostly crocodiles and hippos, while enjoying a rich number of bird-life found in the park.   
* **Accommodation** Overnight at **Nyerere Tented Camp**   
* **Meals:** lunch and dinner


**Day 2: Full Day Game Drive at Nyerere National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive:**  Explore the Nyerere National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, and more.  
* **Accommodation** Overnight at **Nyerere Tented Camp**   
* **Meals:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 3: Early Morning Walking Safari at Nyerere NP and Depart to Mikumi National Park** 

* **Walking Safari:** An early morning walking safari \- creep silently along paths swept by the feet of large animals to discover secret watering holes. Watch as families of elephant wallow and play entirely oblivious to your presence.  
* **Departure to Mikumi:** Depart to Mikumi National Park via Town of Morogoro arriving in Mikumi in the early evening (approx. 5 \- 6 hours’ drive).   
* **Accommodation** Overnight at **Camp Bastian**   
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 4: Full Day Game Drive in Mikumi National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Explore Mikumi National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation** Overnight at **Camp Bastian**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 5: Morning Game Drive in Mikumi NP, Catch SGR Train and Depart to Dar es Salaam**

* **Morning Game Drive:** After breakfast, depart for a morning game drive in Mikumi National Park until noon.  
* **Transfer to SGR Train Station:** Later, transfer to SGR Train Station (approx. 2 hours’ drive) to catch your train at 16:25 hrs and depart to Dar es Salaam, arriving at 18:10 hrs.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
