  
**2 Days/1 Nights Escape to Mikumi National Park**

**Day 1: Catch SGR Train and Depart to Mikumi National Park for A Game Drive**

* **Depart to Morogoro:** Catch an earliest SGR Train at 6:00 am, and depart to Town of Morogoro, arriving at 07:40 am.  
* **Departure to Mikumi:** On arrival, picked up by your driver-guide and depart to Mikumi National (approx. 2 hours’ drive).  
* **Game Drive:** Explore Mikumi National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Camp Bastian**  
* **Meal Plan:** lunch and dinner

**Day 2: Morning Game Drive in Mikumi NP, Catch SGR Train and Depart to Dar es Salaam**

* **Morning Game Drive:** After breakfast, depart for a morning game drive in Mikumi National Park until noon.  
* **Transfer to SGR Train Station:** Later, transfer to SGR Train Station (approx. 2 hours’ drive) to catch your train at 16:25 hrs and depart to Dar es Salaam, arriving at 18:10 hrs.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
