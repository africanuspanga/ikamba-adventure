**10 Days/9 Nights Mikumi National Park, Ruaha National Park, Isimila Stone Age Site, Udzungwa Mountains & Nyerere National Park**

**Day 1: Catch SGR Train and Depart to Mikumi National Park for An Afternoon Game Drive**

* **SGR Train to Morogoro:** Catch an earliest SGR Train at 8:00 am, and depart to Town of Morogoro, arriving at 09;25 am.  
* **Departure to Mikumi:** On arrival at Morogoro Train Station, picked up by your driver-guide and depart to Mikumi National (approx. 2 hours’ drive) arriving for lunch.  
* **Afternoon Game Drive:** Explore Mikumi National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Camp Bastian**  
* **Meal Plan:** lunch and dinner

**Day 2: Morning Game Drive at Mikumi and Depart to Ruaha National Park**

* **Morning Game Drive:** Depart for a morning game drive at Mikumi National Park until noon  
* **Departure to Ruaha National Park:** Later, depart to Ruaha National Park (approx. 5 \- 6 hours’ drive) arriving in the late evening.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Ruaha Hill Top Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 3: Full Day Game Drive at Ruaha National Park** 

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Explore Ruaha National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, herds of elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Ruaha Hill Top Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 4: Full Day Game Drive at Ruaha National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive:** You’ll enjoy another full day game drive at the enormous Ruaha National Park until sunset.   
* **Accommodation**: Overnight at **Ruaha Hill Top Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 5: Visit Isimila Stone Age Historical Site and Depart to Udzungwa Mountains National Park**

* **Depart to Isimila Stone Age Site:** Drive for about 2 hours to Isimila Historical Site  
* **Isimila Stone Age Site:** It’s one of Tanzania’s most fascinating archaeological treasures. Here, guided by an expert, explore ancient sandstone pillars and rock formations that date back hundreds of thousands of years, offering a rare glimpse into early human history.  
* **Depart to Udzungwa Mountains:** Later, depart to Udzungwa Mountains (approx. 5 hours’ drive)  
* **Accommodation**: Overnight at **Udzungwa Falls Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 6: Trekking Udzungwa Mountains, Hike to the Popular Sanje Waterfalls & Depart to Morogoro**

* **A Hike to Sanje Waterfalls:** Hiking through the beautiful forest trails of the Udzungwa Mountains to Sanje Waterfalls. Udzungwa is the critical habitat for unique primates like the Udzungwa red colobus monkey and Sanje crested mangabey, the park is renowned for its spectacular mountains’ scenery, grasslands, rocks, forests and its dramatic waterfalls.  
* **Departure to Morogoro:** In the late afternoon, depart to Town of Morogoro (approx. 2 – 3 hours drive), arriving in the early evening.   
* **Accommodation:** Transfer to the lodge, and spend the overnight at **Forest Sunset Hotel**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner  

**Day 7: Depart to Nyerere National Park (Selous) and An Evening Boat Safari**

* **Departure to Nyerere National Park:** Depart to Nyerere National Park (approx. 4 \- 5 hours’ drive) arriving in the afternoon.  
* **Evening Boat Safari:** Explore the Rufiji River through a fascinating boat safari experience to encounter the aquatic animals, mostly crocodiles and hippos, while enjoying a rich number of bird-life found in the park.   
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Nyerere Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner


**Day 8: Full Day Game Drive at Nyerere National Park (Selous Game Reserve)**

* **Full Day Game Drive:**  Explore Nyerere National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Nyerere Tented Camp**   
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 9: Morning Walking Safari & A Full Day Game Drive at Nyerere National Park**

* **Morning Walking Safari:** Creep silently along paths swept by the feet of large animals to discover secret watering holes, watch as families of elephant wallow and play entirely oblivious to your presence.  
* **Full Day Game Drive:** After breakfast at the camp, depart for another full day game drive across the enormous Nyerere National Park to one again experience its rich wildlife until sunset  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Nyerere Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 10: Maasai Cultural Tour & Departure to Dar es Salaam**

* **Maasai Cultural Tour:** This enriching cultural experience offers a glimpse into the traditional lifestyle of the Maasai people. You'll learn about their customs, dress, and social structure, and have the chance to interact with community members in an authentic setting.  
* **Departure to Dar es Salaam:** Depart back to Dar es Salaam (approx. 3 \- 4 hours’ drive) arriving in the early afternoon.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch

**\-END OF TOUR-**
