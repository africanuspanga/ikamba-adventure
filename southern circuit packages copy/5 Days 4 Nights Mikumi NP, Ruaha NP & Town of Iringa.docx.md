**5 Days/4 Nights \- Mikumi National Park, Ruaha National Park & Town of Iringa**

**Day 1: Catch SGR Train and Depart to Mikumi National Park for An Afternoon Game Drive**

* **Depart to Morogoro:** Catch SGR Train at 8:00 am, and depart to Town of Morogoro, arriving at 09:30 am.  
* **Departure to Mikumi:** Upon arrival at Morogoro Station, picked up by your driver-guide and depart to Mikumi National (approx. 2 hours’ drive).  
* **Afternoon Game Drive:** Explore Mikumi National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Camp Bastian**  
* **Meal Plan:** lunch and dinner 

**Day 2: Morning Game Drive at Mikumi and Depart to Ruaha National Park**

* **Morning Game Drive:** Depart for a morning game drive at Mikumi National Park until early noon  
* **Departure to Ruaha National Park:** Later, depart to Ruaha National Park (approx. 6 hours’ drive) arriving in the late evening.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Ruaha Hill Top Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 3: Full Day Game Drive at Ruaha National Park** 

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Explore Ruaha National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, herds of elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Ruaha Hill Top Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 4: Full Day Game Drive at Ruaha National Park and Depart to Town of Iringa**

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Experience and enjoy another full day game drive at the enormous Ruaha National Park until early evening.   
* Departure to Iringa: Depart to Town of Iringa (approx. 2 – 3 hours drive), arriving in the late evening, and transferred to a hotel.  
* **Accommodation**: Overnight at **Iringa Sunset Hotel**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 5: Depart to Morogoro, Catch SGR Train and Depart to Dar es Salaam**

* **Depart to Morogoro:** Depart to Town of Morogoro (approx. 5 – 6 hours’ drive)  
* **SGR Train Station and Depart to Dar es Salaam:** Upon arrival at Morogoro, catch your train at 16:25 hrs. and depart to Dar es Salaam, arriving at 18:10 hrs., and transferred to your hotel.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
