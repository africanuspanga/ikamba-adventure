**3 Days/2 Nights Mikumi National Park**

**Day 1: Catch SGR Train, Depart to Mikumi National Park and An Afternoon Game Drive**

* **SGR Train to Morogoro:** Catch an earliest SGR Train at 8:00 am, and depart to Town of Morogoro, arriving at 9:30 am.  
* **Departure to Mikumi:** On arrival, picked up by your driver-guide and depart to Mikumi National (approx. 2 hours’ drive) arriving for lunch.  
* **Afternoon Game Drive:** Explore Mikumi National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Camp Bastian**   
* **Meals:** lunch and dinner 

**Day 2: Full Day Game Drive at Mikumi National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Embark on a full-day game drive through Mikumi National Park. The park’s expansive Mkata floodplain is renowned for its abundant wildlife and open vistas, making it ideal for game viewing and photography.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight stay at **Camp Bastian**   
* **Meals:** breakfast, lunch and dinner 


**Day 3: Maasai Cultural Tour, Catch SGR Train and Depart to Dar es Salaam**

* **Maasai Cultural Tour:** This enriching cultural experience offers a glimpse into the traditional lifestyle of the Maasai people. You'll learn about their customs, dress, and social structure, and have the chance to interact with community members in an authentic setting.  
* **Transfer to SGR Train Station:** Later, transfer to SGR Train Station (approx. 2 hours’ drive) to catch your train at 16:25 hrs and depart to Dar es Salaam, arriving at 18:10 hrs.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
