**5 Days/4 Nights Saadani National Park & Mikumi National Park**

**Day 1: Depart to Saadani National Park and A Game Drive**

* **Departure to Saadani:** Depart from Dar es Salaam at 7:00 am and drive to Saadani National Park (approx. 3 \- 4 hours’ drive).   
* **Game Drive:** Being the only National Park that harbors both marine and terrestrial habitats, Saadani has plenty of marine life and wild animals the only Tanzania Park to protect the mangrove forest with beautiful unspoiled beach that lies within the park.  
* **Accommodation** Overnight at **Saadani TANAPA Cottages**  
* **Meals:** lunch and dinner


**Day 2: Morning Game Drive and An Evening Boat Safari at Saadani National Park**

* **Morning Game Drive:**  Depart for a morning game drive through this unique destination that lies along the Indian ocean to enjoy and experience its wildlife until afternoon.  
* **Evening Boat Safari:** Explore the Wami River through a fascinating boat safari experience to encounter the aquatic animals, mostly crocodiles and hippos, while enjoying a rich number of bird-life that lies along the beautiful forest of mangrove trees of the Indian ocean.  
* **Accommodation** Overnight at **Saadani TANAPA Cottages**  
* **Meals:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 3: En-Route Game Drive while Departing to Mikumi National Park** 

* **En-Route Game Drive and Depart to Mikumi:** Enjoy an en-route game drive while exiting the park of Saadani and experience a scenic drive while departing to Mikumi via Town of Morogoro (approx. 6 \- 7 hours’ drive), arriving in the early evening.  
* **Accommodation** Overnight at **Camp Bastian**   
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 4: Full Day Game Drive in Mikumi National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Explore Mikumi National Park and encounter wildlife such as buffaloes, giraffes, elephants, zebras, lions, wildebeests, antelope and more.  
* **Accommodation** Overnight at **Camp Bastian**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 5: Morning Game Drive at Mikumi NP, Catch SGR Train and Depart to Dar es Salaam**

* **Morning Game Drive:** After breakfast, depart for a morning game drive in Mikumi National Park until noon.  
* **Transfer to SGR Train Station:** Later, transfer to SGR Train Station (approx. 2 hours’ drive) to catch your train at 16:25 hrs and depart to Dar es Salaam, arriving at 18:10 hrs.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
